Manufacturing Facility

Enriching Human lives through applied innovation

At our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility at Roorkee, Uttarakhand, We cater to OSD requirements for our domestic, institutional and export markets. We are committed to producing high-quality pharmaceutical products that meet the needs of our customers and improve the lives of patients around the world.

Our manufacturing unit has been aggregated by WHO, NAFDAC, PHILIPINES, GHANA, KENYA, ZIMBABWE, and SRILANKA, UGANDA.

Pulse has always been a pioneer in embracing new technologies

We are dedicated to ensuring that our facilities are equipped with the latest technology and all our technical operations are overseen and managed by seasoned professionals with years of experience in plant operations in the highly regulated pharmaceutical manufacturing sector.
